### Overview:
AYURE (Originally "Illuma") approached the team I was apart of at ECOM DEPT with little to work with but a strong vision of their ideas. It was a pleasure to be the only developer bringing this brand to life for the very first time.

![AYURE Mockup 1](../ayure-1.png)
![AYURE Mockup 2](../ayure-2.png)

### Challenges:
- Completely from scratch, empty store, not many products or content to work with
- Client changed their minds quite a bit over the course of the development phase
- Deadlines were tight despite the frequently changing scope

### Stack:
Shopify Plus, Vanilla JS, CSS, HTML
